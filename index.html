ABOUT

My name is Olivia Carey and I am an aerial artist based in Ottawa, Ontario. I started training in dance and acrobatics from a young age and later developed a passion for teaching and performing aerial arts. My main performance art is aerial hoop (a round, metal hoop that hangs from the ceiling). I have performed at a number of local corporate and charity events in Ottawa.

I would love to speak with you about making your next event special with an aerial performance.

WHY HIRE AN AERIALIST?

Hiring a skilled aerialist to perform at your event or venue is sure to woo any audience, add something special to your event or keep clients coming back.

Aerial performances are a spectacular addition to:
  • Corporate events
  • Private parties
  • Weddings
  • Charity events
  • Festivals
  • bars and clubs
  • and more!

DETAILS

I can rig my hoop to a ceiling or provide my own standing rig for indoor or outdoor use. Rates are available upon inquiry.
